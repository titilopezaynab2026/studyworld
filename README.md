Economics Knowledge Hub 📈

A curated repository of economics concepts, theories, data resources, case studies, research tools, and practical applications designed for students, professionals, and lifelong learners.

🎯 Purpose

Economics influences nearly every aspect of modern life—from personal finance and business decisions to government policies and global markets.

This repository serves as a centralized learning hub where students can:

Understand economic principles from beginner to advanced levels.
Explore real-world applications of economic theories.
Learn how economists analyze markets and public policy.
Access high-quality learning resources, datasets, and research tools.
Build a strong foundation for careers in economics, finance, consulting, public policy, and data analysis.

⭐ Repository Vision

"Learn economics not just to understand markets, but to understand how the world works."

This repository aims to bridge the gap between academic economics and practical decision-making, creating a resource that remains valuable long after graduation.

Unique Addition (Highly Recommended)

Create a folder called:

/economics-in-the-news

Every week, analyze one current news story and explain it using economic concepts. Over time, this becomes a portfolio showing not only what you've learned but how you apply economics to real-world events—a feature that stands out to professors, recruiters, and employers.
